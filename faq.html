Frequently Asked Questions

What is an NFT, and how is it different from a traditional photograph or artwork?

An NFT, or non-fungible token, is a unique digital asset that is verified using blockchain technology. Unlike a traditional photograph or artwork, an NFT cannot be replicated or duplicated.

How do I purchase an NFT from your store?

To purchase an NFT from our store, simply browse our selection and select the item you are interested in. Follow the checkout process and pay using the accepted cryptocurrency.

What is the advantage of owning an NFT over a traditional photograph or artwork?

Owning an NFT gives you exclusive ownership of a digital asset that cannot be replicated. Additionally, the value of NFTs can appreciate over time, potentially providing a profitable investment opportunity.

What happens if I lose my NFT?

Losing your NFT can result in the loss of your ownership rights and the ability to sell or transfer the asset. We recommend keeping your NFT in a secure digital wallet to prevent loss.

How can I ensure the authenticity of an NFT?

Our NFTs are verified using blockchain technology, which provides a transparent and immutable record of ownership. You can rest assured that the NFT you purchase from our store is authentic and verified.
